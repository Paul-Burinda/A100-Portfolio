THE PROGRAM

Join Our Software Development Training Program

Are you passionate about software development and looking to get involved in the CT tech scene? A100 offers applicants the opportunity to both attain the technical ability to write software, as well as develop interpersonal skills that startups seek in their team members.

Learn the sought after software development skills

Apprentices are trained according to guidelines set up by the same partner companies that are looking to hire by the end of the program. Throughout the applicants will become familiar with modern web development, the agile development philosophy and scrum, mobile development, and much more.

Get paired up with employers

A100 works to match its apprentices up with their ideal position on the new haven tech. A100 alumni have been successfully matched with paid jobs at companies such as Independent Software, Broadstripes, MediaCrossing, and ActualFood.

      ABOUT US

      Why A100?
      In Connecticut local employers lack the ability to match software development talent and computer science students to the local jobs they are looking to fill. In addition, students in the area are not always fully aware or prepared for the available jobs. Through A100 we recruit students or graduates who want to stay in connecticut, and through our 2 ½ month free training, we teach them how to work in Agile teams, prepare their skills to the needs of the employer, and then we match them up with local companies and startups who are seeking developers.
